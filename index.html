About Edward Kings

Nurturing the Next Generation

Edward Kings Academy is more than just a school; it's a sanctuary for the curious mind and a community dedicated to holistic growth.

Located in the heart of Jomvu-Jitoni, Mombasa, we provide a safe and stimulating environment where Curiosity meets Knowledge. Our team of certified educators is committed to delivering the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) with a global perspective.
